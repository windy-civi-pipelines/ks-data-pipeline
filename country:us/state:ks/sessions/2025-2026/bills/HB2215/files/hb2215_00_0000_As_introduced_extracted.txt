Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Corrections and Juvenile Justice
Requested by Jennifer King on behalf of the Department of Corrections
2-3
1 AN ACT concerning the department of corrections; relating to public-
2 private partnership construction projects; modifying the definition of
3 public-private partnerships to increase the allowable cost-share limit for
4 expenditures by the department of corrections on such construction
5 projects; amending K.S.A. 2024 Supp. 75-52,167 and repealing the
6 existing section.
7
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
9 Section 1. K.S.A. 2024 Supp. 75-52,167 is hereby amended to read as
10 follows: 75-52,167. As used in K.S.A. 75-3739, and amendments thereto,
11 and K.S.A. 2024 Supp. 75-52,167 through 75-52,171, and amendments
12 thereto:
13 (a) "Private entity" means any partnership, firm, association,
14 corporation, sole proprietorship or other business organization, whether
15 organized for profit or not-for-profit and includes any faith-based
16 organization.
17 (b) "Secretary" means the secretary of corrections.
18 (c) "Public-private partnership" means the relationship established
19 between the department of corrections and a private entity by contracting
20 for the performance of any combination of specified functions or
21 responsibilities to develop, finance, construct or renovate a building at a
22 correctional institution where the department of corrections cost for
23 development, finance, construction or renovation of such building does not
24 exceed 25% 50% of the total cost of the developing, financing,
25 constructing or renovating such building.
26 (d) "Correctional institution" means the same as defined in K.S.A.
27 75-5202, and amendments thereto.
28 (e) "Public-private project" means the project to develop, finance,
29 construct or renovate a building at a correctional institution pursuant to a
30 public-private partnership.
31 (f) "Faith-based organization" means any religious, charitable or other
32 organization described in article 17 of chapter 17 of the Kansas Statutes
33 Annotated, and amendments thereto, or any other organization whose
34 values are based on faith and beliefs, or both, that has a mission based on
35 social values of the particular faith and whose members are from a
HB 2215 2
1 particular faith group.
2 (g) "Spiritual needs" means any program or service that addresses any
3 issue related to sincerely held religious beliefs.
4 Sec. 2. K.S.A. 2024 Supp. 75-52,167 is hereby repealed.
5 Sec. 3. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
6 publication in the statute book.
