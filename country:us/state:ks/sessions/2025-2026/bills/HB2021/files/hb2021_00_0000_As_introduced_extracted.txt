Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Elections
Requested by Representative Waggoner
1-16
1 AN ACT concerning elections; relating to advance voting ballots;
2 authorizing the secretary of state to adopt rules and regulations for the
3 use of remote ballot boxes.
4
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
6 Section 1. (a) On or before January 1, 2026, the secretary of state,
7 after consultation with county election officers, shall adopt rules and
8 regulations for the use of remote ballot boxes by county election officers
9 for the return of advance voting ballots. Such regulations shall include the
10 dates, times and locations for the use of remote ballot boxes and any other
11 requirements that the secretary deems necessary to preserve the integrity
12 of the ballots, the remote ballot boxes and the election process.
13 (b) As used in this section, "remote ballot boxes" means any form of
14 locked ballot boxes designated by the county election officer as available
15 for use for depositing advance voting ballots. "Remote ballot boxes" does
16 not include any ballot boxes located in a county election office or satellite
17 advance voting site.
18 Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
19 publication in the statute book.
