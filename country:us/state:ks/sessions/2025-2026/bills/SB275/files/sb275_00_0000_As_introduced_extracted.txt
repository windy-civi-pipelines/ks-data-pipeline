Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Federal and State Affairs
2-18
1 AN ACT concerning school districts; relating to certain curriculum;
2 requiring the inclusion of a human fetal development presentation as
3 part of the curriculum for any course that addresses human growth,
4 human development or human sexuality.
5
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
7 Section 1. Any school district that offers any course or other
8 instruction that addresses human growth, human development or human
9 sexuality shall include, as a part of such course or instruction, a
10 presentation of a high-quality, computer-generated animation or high-
11 definition ultrasound of at least three minutes in duration that shows the
12 development of the brain, heart and other vital organs in early human fetal
13 development.
14 Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
15 publication in the statute book.
