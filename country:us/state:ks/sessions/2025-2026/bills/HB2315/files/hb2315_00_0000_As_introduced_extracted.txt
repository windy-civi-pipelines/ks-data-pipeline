Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Health and Human Services
Requested by Representative McDonald on behalf of Kathryn Meinhardt of Dream
Acres FASD Community and Kansas FASD Support Network
2-6
1 AN ACT concerning days of commemoration; designating every
2 September 9 as Kansas fetal alcohol spectrum disorders day.
3
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
5 Section 1. Every September 9 shall be designated as Kansas fetal
6 alcohol syndrome disorders day to:
7 (a) Promote awareness of prenatal exposure to alcohol;
8 (b) increase compassion for individuals affected by prenatal exposure
9 to alcohol; and
10 (c) ensure healthier communities across Kansas.
11 Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
12 publication in the statute book.
