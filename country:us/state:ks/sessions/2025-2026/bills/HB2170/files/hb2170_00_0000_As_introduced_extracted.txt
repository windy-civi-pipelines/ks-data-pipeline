Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Health and Human Services
Requested by Representative Essex
1-30
1 AN ACT concerning days of commemoration; designating the first week
2 of February as Kansas burn awareness week.
3
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
5 Section 1. The first full week of February shall be designated as
6 "Kansas burn awareness week" to accomplish all of the following:
7 (a) Commemorate a week of remembrance for all who have lost their
8 lives or have been injured as a result of a burn;
9 (b) raise awareness for the seriousness of accidental burn injuries and
10 fatalities in this state; and
11 (c) educate the public on effective preventative measures to avoid
12 accidental burn injuries or fatalities.
13 Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
14 publication in the statute book.
