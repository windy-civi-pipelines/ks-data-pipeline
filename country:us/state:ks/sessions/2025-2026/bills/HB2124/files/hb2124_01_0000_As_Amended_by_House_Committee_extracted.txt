Section 1:
As Amended by House Committee
Session of 2025
By Representative Hoffman
1-28
1 AN ACT concerning roads and highways; designating a portion of United
2 States highway 160 as the CPL Monte Wayne Forrest memorial
3 highway, a portion of United States highway 77 as the POW MIA
4 memorial highway and bridge No. 82-14-6.88 (026) in Clay county
5 as the POW MIA memorial bridge.
6
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
8 Section 1. The portion of United States highway 160 from the eastern
9 city limits of Argonia, then east on United States highway 160 to its
10 junction with Eden road is hereby designated as the CPL Monte Wayne
11 Forrest memorial highway. Upon compliance with K.S.A. 8-10,114 68-
12 10,114, and amendments thereto, the secretary of transportation shall place
13 highway signs along the highway right-of-way at proper intervals to
14 indicate that the highway is the CPL Monte Wayne Forrest memorial
15 highway.
16 Sec. 2. The portion of United States highway 77 from its junction
17 with interstate 70, then north on United States highway 77 in Geary
18 county to its western junction with United States highway 24 in Riley
19 county, then north on United States highway 77 from its eastern
20 junction with United States highway 24 to the junction with K-16
21 highway is hereby designated as the POW MIA memorial highway.
22 Upon compliance with K.S.A. 68-10,114, and amendments thereto, the
23 secretary of transportation shall place suitable signs along the
24 highway right-of-way at proper intervals to indicate that the highway
25 is the POW MIA memorial highway.
26 Sec. 3. Bridge No. 82-14-6.88 (026) located on K-82 highway in
27 Clay county is hereby designated as the POW MIA memorial bridge.
28 Upon compliance with K.S.A. 68-10,114, and amendments thereto, the
29 secretary of transportation shall place suitable signs to indicate that
30 such bridge is the POW MIA memorial bridge.
31 Sec. 2. 4. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
32 publication in the statute book.
