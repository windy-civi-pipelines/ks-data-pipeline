Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Veterans and Military
Requested by Representative Butler
2-4
1 AN ACT concerning the governor; creating the order of the sunflower;
2 directing the governor to award the order of the sunflower to recognize
3 military spouses' service to their communities.
4
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
6 Section 1. (a) The order of the sunflower shall be awarded to military
7 spouses who have served their communities with distinction.
8 (b) The award recipient shall have been deemed to have made a
9 significant impact on their unity command, military installation,
10 community or the state of Kansas.
11 (c) The governor shall award the order of the sunflower based upon
12 submissions made to the governor's office through constituent services on
13 the governor's website. Award recommendations shall provide the dates of
14 service, significant accomplishments and recommendations from the unit
15 command.
16 Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
17 publication in the statute book.
