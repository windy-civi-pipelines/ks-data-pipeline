Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Representative Rhiley
1-30
1 AN ACT enacting the returning to nonaccountability of the executive
2 branch agencies that report to the governor act; eliminating the budget
3 process requirements of a program service inventory, integrated budget
4 fiscal process and performance-based budgeting system; repealing
5 K.S.A. 75-3718b.
6
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
8 Section 1. K.S.A. 75-3718b is hereby repealed.
9 Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
10 publication in the statute book.
