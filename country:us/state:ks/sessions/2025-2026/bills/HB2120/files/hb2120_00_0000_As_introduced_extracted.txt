By Representative Roeser
1-28
1 AN ACT concerning the disposition of state real property; authorizing the
2 state board of
3 regents on behalf of Kansas state university veterinary medical center
4 to sell certain real property in the city of Omaha, Douglas county,
5 Nebraska; authorizing the state board of regents on behalf of Kansas
6 state university to sell certain real property in the city of Manhattan,
7 Riley county, Kansas.
8
9 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
10 Section 1. (a) The state board of regents is hereby authorized and
11 empowered, for and on behalf of Kansas state university veterinary
12 medical center, to sell and convey all of the rights, title and interest subject
13 to all easements and appurtenances in the following described real estate,
14 located at 9706 Mockingbird Drive, Omaha, Douglas county, Nebraska,
15 and further described as:
16 A part of Lot 555, in Mockingbird Hills West, an addition to the City of
17 Omaha, as surveyed, platted and recorded in Douglas County, Nebraska,
18 (sometimes referred to as Parcel 15A), being more particularly described
19 as follows:
20 Commencing at the Southeast property corner of Lot 555; thence South
21 89°17'33" West (assumed bearing), on the South line of said Lot 555, (said
22 line also being the Northerly right-of-way line of Mockingbird Drive), a
23 distance of 706.00 feet, to the Point of Beginning; thence continuing along
24 said South line of Lot 555, a distance of 4.64 feet, to a point of curvature,
25 thence Northwesterly along a 932.78 foot radius curve to the right, an arc
26 distance of 206.80 feet; thence North 00°42'27" West, a distance of 216.72
27 feet; thence Nort 89°17'33" East, a distance of 209.75 feet; thence South
28 00°42'27" East, a distance of 239.56 feet, to the point of beginning;
29 Together with non-exclusive easement rights reserved in instrument
30 dated October 29, 1981 and recorded October 30, 1981 in Book 1678 at
31 Page 35 of the Deed Records of Douglas County, Nebraska.
32 (b) Conveyance of such rights, title and interest in such real estate
33 shall be executed in the name of the state board of regents by its
34 chairperson and executive officer. All proceeds from the sale and
35 conveyance thereof shall be deposited in the restricted fees fund (368-00-
36 2590-5530) of Kansas state university veterinary medical center.

2 made or accepted by the state board of regents until the deeds, titles and

5 description of the real estate described in this section is incorrect, the state
