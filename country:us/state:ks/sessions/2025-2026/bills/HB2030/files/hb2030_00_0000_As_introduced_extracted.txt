Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Transportation
Requested by Representative Hoheisel on behalf of Catalyst
1-17
1 AN ACT concerning motor vehicles; relating to the vehicle dealers and
2 manufacturers licensing act; excluding dealers and manufacturers of
3 trailers from certain provisions thereof; exceptions thereto.
4
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
6 Section 1. (a) The provisions of K.S.A. 8-2414 through 8-2416, 8-
7 2419, 8-2430, 8-2431, 8-2438, 8-2439, 8-2445 and 8-2446, and
8 amendments thereto, shall not apply to dealers and manufacturers of
9 trailers, except that such provisions shall apply to dealers and
10 manufacturers of semitrailers and travel trailers as those terms are defined
11 in K.S.A. 8-126, and amendments thereto.
12 (b) The provisions of this section shall be a part of and supplemental
13 to the vehicle dealers and manufacturers licensing act.
14 Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
15 publication in the statute book.
