Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Public Health and Welfare
2-3
1 AN ACT concerning public health; relating to schools and child care
2 facilities; prohibiting a school or such facility from excluding a child
3 who has been exposed to an infectious or contagious disease without a
4 valid isolation or quarantine order; amending K.S.A. 65-122 and
5 repealing the existing section.
6
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
8 Section 1. K.S.A. 65-122 is hereby amended to read as follows: 65-
9 122. No A person afflicted with an infectious or contagious disease
10 dangerous to the public health shall not be admitted into any public,
11 parochial or private school or licensed child care facility. It shall be the
12 duty of the parent or guardian, and the principal or other person in charge
13 of any public, parochial, private school or licensed child care facility to
14 exclude therefrom any child or other person affected with a disease that is
15 suspected of being infectious or contagious until the expiration of the
16 prescribed period of isolation or quarantine for the particular infectious or
17 contagious disease. If the attending person licensed to practice medicine
18 and surgery a physician or local health officer finds upon examination that
19 the person affected with a disease, that is suspected of being infectious or
20 contagious is not suffering from an infectious or contagious disease, he or
21 she such physician may submit a certificate to this effect to the person in
22 charge of the public, parochial, private school or licensed child care
23 facility and such person shall be readmitted to school or to the child care
24 facility.
25 (b) A person who has been exposed or potentially exposed to an
26 infectious or contagious disease shall not be excluded from a public,
27 parochial or private school or licensed child care facility due to such
28 exposure or potential exposure unless the local health office or secretary
29 has issued an order to such person requiring isolation or quarantine
30 pursuant to K.S.A. 65-129c, and amendments thereto. Any exclusion from
31 school due to exposure or potential exposure to an infectious or
32 contagious disease shall not be longer than the period of isolation or
33 quarantine specified in such order.
34 (c) As used in this section, "physician" means a person licensed to
35 practice medicine and surgery by the state board of healing arts.
36 Sec. 2. K.S.A. 65-122 is hereby repealed.
SB 172 2
1 Sec. 3. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
2 publication in the statute book.
