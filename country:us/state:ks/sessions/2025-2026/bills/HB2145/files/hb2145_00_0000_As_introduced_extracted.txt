Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Local Government
Requested by Representative Blex
1-29
1 AN ACT concerning fairs; relating to certain county fair boards;
2 establishing the membership for the Butler county fair board; providing
3 for the appointment of members thereto; allowing up to five members
4 of such board to be appointed from the county at large; amending
5 K.S.A. 19-2699 and repealing the existing section.
6
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
8 Section 1. K.S.A. 19-2699 is hereby amended to read as follows: 19-
9 2699. (a) The county fair board of Cloud county shall consist of 12
10 members. An annual meeting for appointment of members of the fair
11 board to succeed members with expiring terms shall be conducted in
12 December by the presiding county commissioners. At the annual
13 appointment meeting, one member of the board shall be appointed, if
14 possible, from each county commissioner district and one member shall be
15 appointed from the county at large. Any person of legal voting age and
16 residing in the county shall be eligible for membership on the board.
17 (b) The county fair board of Butler county shall consist of 15
18 members. An annual meeting for appointment of members of the fair board
19 to succeed members with expiring terms shall be conducted in December
20 by the presiding county commissioners. At the annual appointment
21 meeting, one member of the board shall be appointed, if possible, from
22 each county commissioner district and not more than five members may be
23 appointed from the county at large. Any person of legal voting age and
24 residing in the county shall be eligible for membership on the board.
25 (c) Except as hereinafter otherwise provided, the term of office of
26 each member of such fair boards shall be three years commencing January
27 1 and ending December 31. Members of the each such board shall be
28 eligible to serve unlimited consecutive terms. Vacancies in the membership
29 of the each such board shall be filled by appointment by the remaining
30 members of the such board for the unexpired term of office. Members of
31 the each board shall elect from among its own members a president, vice-
32 president vice president, secretary and treasurer from among the members
33 of such board.
34 Sec. 2. K.S.A. 19-2699 is hereby repealed.
35 Sec. 3. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
HB 2145 2
1 publication in the statute book.
