Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Commerce, Labor and Economic Development
Requested by Clay Barker on behalf of the Office of the Secretary of State
1-28
1 AN ACT concerning consumer protection; requiring any person who
2 solicits a fee for filing or retrieving certain documents from the federal
3 government, the state, a state agency or a local government to give
4 certain notices to consumers; providing that violation of such
5 requirements is a deceptive act or practice subject to penalties under the
6 Kansas consumer protection act.
7
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
9 Section 1. (a) This section applies to any person other than the federal
10 government, the state, a state agency or a local government who solicits a
11 fee for filing a document with, or retrieving a copy or certified copy of a
12 certificate or public record from, the federal government, the state, a state
13 agency or a local government.
14 (b) A person who solicits as described in subsection (a) shall include
15 in each solicitation:
16 (1) A statement in the solicitation, in the same language as the
17 solicitation, that is identical or substantially similar to the following: "This
18 is an advertisement. This offer is not being made by, or on behalf of, any
19 government agency. You are not required to make any payment or take any
20 other action in response to this offer." If the solicitation is in writing, the
21 statement shall be in at least 24-point type and located at the top of the
22 physical document or the beginning of the electronic communication;
23 (2) the name of the person making the solicitation and the person's
24 physical address, which shall not be a post office box;
25 (3) information on where the consumer can file a document directly
26 with the secretary of state or retrieve a copy or certified copy of a
27 certificate or public record; and
28 (4) if the solicitation is mailed, the words "THIS IS NOT A
29 GOVERNMENT DOCUMENT" in 24-point type and all capital letters on
30 the envelope, outside cover or wrapper in which the solicitation is mailed.
31 (c) A solicitation described in subsection (a) shall not be in a form or
32 use deadline dates or other language that makes the document appear to be
33 issued by the federal government, the state, a state agency or a local
34 government, or that appears to impose a legal duty on the person being
35 solicited.
HB 2118 2
1 (d) A violation of this section shall constitute a deceptive act or
2 practice as provided in K.S.A. 50-626, and amendments thereto.
3 (e) As used in this section, "solicit" or "solicitation" means to directly
4 advertise to a person. "Solicit" and "solicitation" do not include:
5 (1) Communication initiated by a consumer; or
6 (2) advertising or marketing to a consumer with whom the solicitor
7 has a current or former commercial relationship.
8 (f) This section shall be a part of and supplemental to the Kansas
9 consumer protection act.
10 Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
11 publication in the statute book.
