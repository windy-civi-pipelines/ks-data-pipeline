1 AN ACT concerning roads and highways; designating a portion of K-5
2 highway as the Representative Rep Marvin S Robinson II memorial
3 highway; redesignating a current portion of the Harry Darby memorial
4 highway for interstate highway 635; amending K.S.A. 68-1037 and
5 repealing the existing section.
6
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
8 New Section 1. The portion of K-5 highway from the junction of K-5
9 highway and north 18th street in Wyandotte county, then west on K-5
10 highway to the junction of north 38th street is hereby designated as the
11 Representative Rep Marvin S Robinson II memorial highway. Upon
12 compliance with K.S.A. 68-10,114, and amendments thereto, the secretary
13 of transportation shall place highway signs along the highway right-of-way
14 at proper intervals to indicate that the highway is the Representative Rep
15 Marvin S Robinson II memorial highway.
16 Sec. 2. K.S.A. 68-1037 is hereby amended to read as follows: 68-
17 1037. Interstate highway 635 where it enters the state on the Kansas-
18 Missouri line on the north, thence south to the eastern junction with K-5
19 highway, thence south from the western junction with K-5 highway to the
20 junction with Interstate highway 35, is hereby designated as the Harry
21 Darby memorial highway. The secretary of transportation shall place
22 markers along the highway right-of-way at proper intervals to indicate that
23 the highway is the Harry Darby memorial highway. The secretary of
HB 2029—Am. by SC 2
1 transportation may accept and administer gifts and donations to aid in
2 obtaining and installing suitable highway signs bearing the proper
3 approved inscription.
4 Sec. 3. K.S.A. 68-1037 is hereby repealed.
5 Sec. 4. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
6 publication in the statute book Kansas register.
