Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Judiciary
Requested by Meredith Fry on behalf of the Kansas Coalition Against Sexual &
Domestic Violence
1-30
1 AN ACT concerning protection orders; prohibiting a sheriff from charging
2 a fee for service of process for proceedings under the protection from
3 abuse act and the protection from stalking, sexual assault or human
4 trafficking act; amending K.S.A. 28-110 and repealing the existing
5 section.
6
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
8 Section 1. K.S.A. 28-110 is hereby amended to read as follows: 28-
9 110. (a) Except as otherwise provided in this section:
10 (1) On and after July 1, 2012 through June 30, 2013, the sheriff of
11 each Kansas county shall charge a fee of $10 for serving, executing and
12 returning any process.
13 (2) On and after July 1, 2013, the sheriff of each Kansas county shall
14 charge a fee of $15 for serving, executing and returning any process,
15 except that no fee shall be charged for serving, executing and returning
16 any process for a proceeding pursuant to the protection from abuse act as
17 described in K.S.A. 60-3104, and amendments thereto, or the protection
18 from stalking, sexual assault and human trafficking act as described in
19 K.S.A. 60-31a04, and amendments thereto.
20 (b) Subject to subsection (e), the fee described in subsection (a) shall
21 be charged for serving, executing and returning process, as well as for any
22 unsuccessful attempts to serve, execute or return process.
23 (c) If more than one process for the same person in the same case is
24 issued and is in the hands of a sheriff at one time, the sheriff shall charge a
25 single fee for serving, executing and returning the processes.
26 (d) If more than one process for different persons at the same address
27 in the same case is issued and is in the hands of a sheriff at one time, the
28 sheriff shall charge a single fee for serving, executing and returning the
29 processes.
30 (e) Where return is not made or timely return is not made pursuant to
31 K.S.A. 60-312 or 61-3005, and amendments thereto, no fee shall be
32 charged for subsequent processes that may be required to effect service
33 and the timely return of the failed service. However, if service is attempted
34 and return is made showing no service because the person to be served
HB 2182 2
1 cannot be served at that address or there is no such address, the fee in
2 subsection (a) shall be charged for an alias summons at the same address.
3 (f) Except as provided by K.S.A. 19-269, and amendments thereto, a
4 sheriff shall be reimbursed for the necessary transportation and board
5 expenses incurred while serving under requisition made by the governor.
6 (g) All fees charged by a sheriff pursuant to this section for the same
7 case may be paid in one combined payment, in a form designated by the
8 sheriff, such as a check or money order.
9 (h) The state of Kansas and all municipalities in this state, as defined
10 in K.S.A. 12-105a, and amendments thereto, are hereby exempt, in any
11 civil action in which such state or municipality is involved, from paying
12 service of process fees prescribed by this section.
13 (i) As used in this section, "process" means any summons, pleading,
14 writ, order or notice issued by a court clerk or court.
15 Sec. 2. K.S.A. 28-110 is hereby repealed.
16 Sec. 3. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
17 publication in the statute book.
