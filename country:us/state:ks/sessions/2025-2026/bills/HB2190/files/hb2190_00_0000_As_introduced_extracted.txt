Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Elections
Requested by Representative Haskins
1-31
1 AN ACT concerning legal public holidays; declaring the even-numbered
2 year general election day as a legal public holiday; amending K.S.A.
3 35-107 and repealing the existing section.
4
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
6 Section 1. K.S.A. 35-107 is hereby amended to read as follows: 35-
7 107. (a) On and after January 1, 2006, The following days are declared to
8 be legal public holidays and are to be observed as such:
9 New Year's Day, January 1;
10 Martin Luther King, Jr. Day, the third Monday in January;
11 President's Day, the third Monday in February;
12 Memorial Day, the last Monday in May;
13 Independence Day, July 4;
14 Labor Day, the first Monday in September;
15 Columbus Day, the second Monday in October;
16 Veterans' Day, the eleventh day in November;
17 Election Day, the Tuesday following the first Monday in even-
18 numbered years;
19 Thanksgiving Day, the fourth Thursday in November;
20 Christmas Day, December 25.
21 (b) Any reference in the laws of this state concerning observance of
22 legal holidays shall on and after January 1, 2006, be considered as a
23 reference to the day or days prescribed in subsection (a) for the observance
24 of such legal holiday or holidays.
25 Sec. 2. K.S.A. 35-107 is hereby repealed.
26 Sec. 3. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
27 publication in the statute book.
