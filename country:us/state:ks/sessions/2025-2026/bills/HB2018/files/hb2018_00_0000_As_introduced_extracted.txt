Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Elections
Requested by Representative Waggoner
1-16
1 AN ACT concerning elections; relating to the qualifications of poll
2 workers; prohibiting the disqualification of active military members,
3 spouses or other dependents as poll workers on the basis of residency
4 or registered voter status.
5
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
7 Section 1. (a) Neither the secretary of state nor any county election
8 officer shall disqualify an individual from serving as a poll worker at any
9 election on the basis of residency or registered voter status if such
10 individual is a citizen of the United States and an active military member
11 or the spouse or other dependent of an active military member.
12 (b) Nothing in this section shall be construed to limit or otherwise
13 restrict any poll worker qualifications based on the age of the individual,
14 except as provided in K.S.A. 25-2804, and amendments thereto.
15 (c) "Active military member" means any person with full-time duty
16 status in the armed forces of the United States, including members of the
17 national guard and reserve.
18 Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
19 publication in the statute book.
