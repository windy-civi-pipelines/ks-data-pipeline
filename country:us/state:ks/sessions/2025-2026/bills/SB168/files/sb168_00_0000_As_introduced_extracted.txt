Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Utilities
2-3
1 AN ACT concerning legislative video recordings; requiring the director of
2 legislative administrative service to remove legislative video recordings
3 from cloud-based servers and video on demand platforms after 10
4 years.
5
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
7 Section 1. (a) The director of legislative administrative services may
8 retain legislative video recordings on any cloud-based server or video on
9 demand platform for a period of 10 years from the date such recording was
10 made. The director of legislative administrative services shall not retain
11 any legislative video recording on any cloud server or video on demand
12 platform beyond such 10-year period and shall remove, or cause to be
13 removed, any such legislative video recording from any cloud-based
14 server or video on demand platform at the end of such 10-year period.
15 (b) As used in this section:
16 (1) "Cloud-based server" means a virtual server operating within a
17 cloud computing environment that allows users to access computing
18 resources such as processing, storage and applications remotely and on
19 demand by a user.
20 (2) "Video on demand platform" means an audiovisual media
21 distribution system that allows users to access audiovisual content and
22 digital media on demand by a user.
23 Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
24 publication in the statute book.
