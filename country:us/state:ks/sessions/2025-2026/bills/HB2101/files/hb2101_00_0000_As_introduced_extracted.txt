Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Welfare Reform
Requested by Representative Awerkamp
1-27
1 AN ACT concerning public assistance; prohibiting cities and counties
2 from adopting or implementing a guaranteed income program;
3 rendering prior adopted programs null and void; defining guaranteed
4 income program.
5
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
7 Section 1. (a) A city or county shall not adopt or enforce an ordinance
8 or resolution, respectively, that establishes or provides for the operation of
9 a guaranteed income program.
10 (b) Any ordinance or resolution prohibited by subsection (a) that was
11 adopted prior to July 1, 2025, shall be null and void.
12 (c) As used in this section, "guaranteed income program" means a
13 program that provides individuals with a regular periodic cash payment
14 that may be used for any purpose without requiring such individuals to be
15 employed, seek employment or attend employment-related training to
16 receive such funds.
17 Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
18 publication in the statute book.
