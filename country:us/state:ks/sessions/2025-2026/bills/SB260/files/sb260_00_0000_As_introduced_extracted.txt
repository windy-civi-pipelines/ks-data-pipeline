Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Ways and Means
2-11
1 AN ACT concerning parimutuel wagering; relating to the Kansas
2 parimutuel racing act; modifying the qualifications for an organization
3 license; redefining horesemen's associations and horsemen's nonprofit
4 organizations; changing the distribution of certain tax revenues;
5 amending K.S.A. 74-8826, 74-8829 and 74-8838 and K.S.A. 2024
6 Supp. 74-8802 and 74-8814 and repealing the existing sections.
7
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
9 Section 1. K.S.A. 2024 Supp. 74-8802 is hereby amended to read as
10 follows: 74-8802. As used in the Kansas parimutuel racing act unless the
11 context otherwise requires:
12 (a) "Breakage" means the odd cents by which the amount payable on
13 each dollar wagered exceeds:
14 (1) A multiple of $.10, for parimutuel pools from races conducted in
15 this state; and
16 (2) a multiple of such other number of cents as provided by law of the
17 host jurisdiction, for interstate combined wagering pools.
18 (b) "Commission" means the Kansas racing and gaming commission
19 created by this act.
20 (c) "Concessionaire licensee" means a person, partnership,
21 corporation or association licensed by the commission to utilize a space or
22 privilege within a racetrack facility to sell goods or services.
23 (d) "Contract" means an agreement, written or oral, between two or
24 more persons, partnerships, corporations or associations, or any
25 combination thereof that creates an obligation between the parties.
26 (e) "Crossover employment" means a situation in which an
27 occupational licensee is concurrently employed at the same racing facility
28 by an organization licensee and a facility owner licensee or facility
29 manager licensee.
30 (f) "Dual racetrack facility" means a racetrack facility for the racing
31 of both horses and greyhounds or two immediately adjacent racetrack
32 facilities, owned by the same licensee, one for racing horses and one for
33 racing greyhounds.
34 (g) "Employee" means a person who has applied for a position of
35 employment or is currently employed by the commission.
36 (h) "Executive director" means the executive director of the
SB 260 2
1 commission.
2 (i) "Facility manager licensee" means a person, partnership,
3 corporation or association licensed by the commission and having a
4 contract with an organization licensee to manage a racetrack facility
5 located in Sedgwick county.
6 (j) "Facility owner licensee" means a person, partnership, corporation
7 or association, or the state of Kansas or any political subdivision thereof,
8 licensed by the commission to construct or own a racetrack facility located
9 in Sedgwick county. "Facility owner licensee" does not mean an
10 organization licensee that owns the racetrack facility in which it conducts
11 horse or greyhound racing.
12 (k) "Fair association" means an association organized pursuant to
13 K.S.A. 2-125 et seq., and amendments thereto or a nonprofit association
14 determined by the commission to be otherwise organized to conduct fair
15 activities pursuant to findings of fact entered by the commission in a
16 license order.
17 (l) "Financial interest" means an interest that could result directly or
18 indirectly in receiving a pecuniary gain or sustaining a pecuniary loss as a
19 result of ownership or interest in a business entity or activity or as a result
20 of a salary, gratuity or other compensation or remuneration from any
21 person.
22 (m) "Greyhound" means any greyhound breed of dog properly
23 registered with the national greyhound association of Abilene, Kansas.
24 (n) "Historical horse race machine" means any electronic,
25 electromechanical, video or computerized device, contrivance or machine
26 authorized by the commission that, upon insertion of cash, tokens,
27 electronic cards or any consideration, is available to accept wagers on and
28 simulate the running of historical horse races, and that may deliver or
29 entitle the patron operating the machine to receive cash, tokens,
30 merchandise or credits that may be redeemed for cash. Historical horse
31 race machines shall use historically accurate information of the horse race
32 selected to determine the place of finish of each horse. No random number
33 generator or other algorithm shall be used for determining the results of an
34 historical horse race. Historical horse race machines shall be directly
35 linked to a central computer at a location determined by the commission
36 for purposes of security, monitoring and auditing.
37 (o) "Horsemen's association" means any association or corporation:
38 (1) All officers, directors, members and shareholders of which are
39 licensed owners of horses or licensed trainers of horses, or both;
40 (2) applying for or has been issued a facility owner license
41 authorizing ownership of Eureka Downs, Anthony Downs or a racetrack
42 facility on or adjacent to premises used by a fair association to conduct fair
43 activities; and
SB 260 3
1 (3) none of the officers, directors, members or shareholders of which
2 holds another facility owner license or is an officer, director, member or
3 shareholder of another facility owner licensee.
4 (p) "Horsemen's nonprofit organization" means any nonprofit
5 organization:
6 (1) All officers, directors, members or shareholders of which are
7 licensed owners of horses or licensed trainers of horses, or both; and
8 (2) applying for or has been issued an organization license
9 authorizing the conduct of horse races at Eureka Downs, Anthony Downs
10 or a racetrack facility on or adjacent to premises used by a fair association
11 to conduct fair activities.
12 (q) "Host facility" means the racetrack at which the race is run or, if
13 the race is run in a jurisdiction that is not participating in the interstate
14 combined wagering pool, the racetrack or other facility that is designated
15 as the host facility.
16 (r) "Host jurisdiction" means the jurisdiction where the host facility is
17 located.
18 (s) "Interstate combined wagering pool" means a parimutuel pool
19 established in one jurisdiction that is combined with comparable
20 parimutuel pools from one or more racing jurisdictions for the purpose of
21 establishing the amount of money returned on a successful wager in the
22 participating jurisdictions.
23 (t) "Intertrack wagering" means wagering on a simulcast race at a
24 licensed racetrack facility or at a facility that is licensed in its racing
25 jurisdiction to conduct live races.
26 (u) "Intrastate combined wagering pool" means a parimutuel pool that
27 is combined with comparable parimutuel pools from one or more racetrack
28 facilities for the purpose of establishing the amount of money returned on
29 a successful wager at the participating racetrack facilities.
30 (v) "Kansas-whelped greyhound" means a greyhound whelped and
31 raised in Kansas for the first six months of its life.
32 (w) "Licensee" means a person who has submitted an application for
33 licesure or currently holds a license issued by the commission.
34 (x) "Minus pool" means a parimutuel pool in which, after deducting
35 the takeout, not enough money remains in the pool to pay the legally
36 prescribed minimum return to those placing winning wagers, and in which
37 the organization licensee would be required to pay the remaining amount
38 due.
39 (y) "Nonprofit organization" means:
40 (1) A corporation that is incorporated in Kansas as a not-for-profit
41 corporation pursuant to the Kansas general corporation code and the net
42 earnings of which do not inure to the benefit of any shareholder, individual
43 member or person; or
SB 260 4
1 (2) a fair association.
2 (z) "Occupation licensee" means a person licensed by the commission
3 to perform an occupation or provide services that the commission has
4 identified as requiring a license pursuant to this act.
5 (aa) "Off-track wagering" means wagering on a simulcast race at a
6 facility that is not licensed in its jurisdiction to conduct live races.
7 (bb) "Organization licensee" means a nonprofit organization licensed
8 by the commission to conduct races pursuant to this act and, if the license
9 so provides, to construct or own a racetrack facility.
10 (cc) "Parimutuel pool" means the total money wagered by individuals
11 on one or more horses or greyhounds in a particular horse or greyhound
12 race to win, place or show, or combinations thereof, as established by the
13 commission, and, except in the case of an interstate or intrastate combined
14 wagering pool, held by the organization licensee pursuant to the
15 parimutuel system of wagering. There is a separate parimutuel pool for
16 win, for place, for show and for each of the other forms of betting
17 provided for by the rules and regulations of the commission.
18 (dd) "Parimutuel wagering" means a form of wagering on the
19 outcome of horse and greyhound races, including historical horse races
20 conducted by an historical horse race machine, in which those who wager
21 purchase tickets of various denominations on one or more horses or
22 greyhounds and all wagers for each race are pooled and the winning ticket
23 holders are paid prizes from such pool in amounts proportional to the total
24 receipts in the pool.
25 (ee) "Race meeting" means one or more periods of racing days during
26 a calendar year designated by the commission for which an organization
27 licensee has been approved by the commission to hold live horse or
28 greyhound races or simulcast horse races at which parimutuel wagering is
29 conducted, including such additional time as designated by the
30 commission for the conduct of official business before and after the races.
31 (ff) "Racetrack facility" means a racetrack within Kansas used for the
32 racing of horses or greyhounds, or both, including the track surface,
33 grandstands, clubhouse, all animal housing and handling areas, other areas
34 in which a person may enter only upon payment of an admission fee or
35 upon presentation of authorized credentials and such additional areas as
36 designated by the commission. The term "racetrack facility" includes a
37 facility used for the display of and wagering on simulcast races and the
38 operation of historical horse race machines without any live horse or
39 greyhound races being conducted.
40 (gg) "Racing jurisdiction" or "jurisdiction" means a governmental
41 authority that is responsible for the regulation of live or simulcast racing in
42 its jurisdiction.
43 (hh) "Racing or wagering equipment or services licensee" means any
SB 260 5
1 person, partnership, corporation or association licensed by the commission
2 to provide integral racing or wagering equipment or services, as designated
3 by the commission, to an organization licensee.
4 (ii) "Recognized greyhound owners' group" means the duly
5 recognized group elected in accordance with rules and regulations of the
6 commission by a majority of the Kansas licensed greyhound owners at the
7 racetrack facility voting in the election. The commission may designate an
8 organization such as the national greyhound association of Abilene,
9 Kansas, to conduct the election.
10 (jj) "Recognized horsemen's group" means the duly recognized
11 group, representing the breeds of horses running at a racetrack facility,
12 elected in accordance with rules and regulations of the commission by a
13 majority of the licensed owners and trainers at the racetrack facility voting
14 in the election. If the licensee does not have a recognized horsemen's
15 group, the commission shall designate as the recognized horsemen's group
16 one that serves another organization licensee, but not one that serves a fair
17 association organization licensee.
18 (kk) "Simulcast" means a live audio-visual broadcast of an actual
19 horse race at the time it is run.
20 (ll) "Takeout" means the total amount of money withheld from each
21 parimutuel pool for the payment of purses, taxes and the share to be kept
22 by the organization licensee. Takeout does not include the breakage. The
23 balance of each pool less the breakage is distributed to the holders of
24 winning parimutuel tickets.

19 (d) Except as otherwise provided by this section, all applicants for
