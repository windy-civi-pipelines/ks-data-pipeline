Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Taxation
Requested by Representative Carlin
2-13
1 AN ACT concerning retirement and pensions; relating to the Kansas public
2 employees retirement system and system thereunder; providing for a
3 postretirement cost-of-living adjustment for certain retirants who are 85
4 years of age or older.
5
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
7 Section 1. (a) The retirement benefit, pension or annuity payments
8 accruing after June 30, 2025, to each retirant shall be increased by a cost-
9 of-living adjustment in an amount as provided in subsection (b). Such
10 payments shall be paid by the retirement system to the retirant.
11 (b) Each retirant who is 85 years of age or older on July 1, 2025, and
12 who is entitled to receive a retirement benefit, pension or annuity payment
13 from the retirement system on July 1, 2025, shall have such retirement
14 benefit, pension or annuity increased by $150 per month of the retirement
15 benefit, pension or annuity payment in effect on July 1, 2025.
16 (c) As used in this section:
17 (1) "Retirant" means any:
18 (A) Person who is a member or special member of the retirement
19 system pursuant to the provisions of K.S.A. 74-4901 et seq., and
20 amendments thereto, and who is 85 years of age or older on July 1, 2025;
21 and
22 (B) person who is a joint annuitant or beneficiary of any member
23 described in subparagraph (A); and
24 (2) "retirement system" means the Kansas public employees
25 retirement system, the Kansas police and firemen's retirement system, the
26 state school retirement system and the retirement system for judges.
27 (d) The provisions of this section shall be a part of and supplemental
28 to K.S.A. 74-4901 et seq., and amendments thereto.
29 Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
30 publication in the statute book.
