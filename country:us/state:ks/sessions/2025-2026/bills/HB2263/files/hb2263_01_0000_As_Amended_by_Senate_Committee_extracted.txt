Section 1:
As Amended by Senate Committee
Session of 2025
By Committee on Transportation
Requested by Catherine Magaña on behalf of the Department of Transportation
2-4
1 AN ACT concerning roads and highways; designating a future interchange
2 on K-10 highway as the Kris Norton memorial interchange.
3
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
5 Section 1. The interchange that will be constructed as part of the K-
6 10 highway expansion project located at the existing at grade intersection
7 of K-10 highway and Wakarusa drive in Douglas county is hereby
8 designated as the Kris Norton memorial interchange. Upon compliance
9 with K.S.A. 68-1,114, and amendments thereto, the secretary of
10 transportation shall place highway signs along the highway right-of-way at
11 proper intervals to indicate that the interchange is the Kris Norton
12 memorial interchange.
13 Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
14 publication in the statute book Kansas register.
