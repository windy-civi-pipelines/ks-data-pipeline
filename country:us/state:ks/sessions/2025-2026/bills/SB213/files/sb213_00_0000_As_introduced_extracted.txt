Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Federal and State Affairs
2-5
1 AN ACT concerning wildlife; relating to hunting; prohibiting nonresidents
2 from hunting migratory waterfowl during the hunting season except on
3 Sundays, Mondays and Tuesdays; applying such restriction to
4 department lands and waters, federal lands and waters managed by the
5 United States army corps of engineers at specified reservoirs, the
6 bureau of reclamation at certain reservoirs and the United States fish
7 and wildlife service at designated refuges; exempting properties
8 designated as walk-in hunting access areas, navigable rivers in Kansas
9 and the conservation order for light geese season from such restriction;
10 reducing fees for migratory waterfowl habitat stamps; amending K.S.A.
11 32-939 and K.S.A. 2024 Supp. 32-988 and repealing the existing
12 sections.
13
14 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
15 Section 1. K.S.A. 32-939 is hereby amended to read as follows: 32-
16 939. (a) As used in this section,:
17 (1) "Department lands and waters" means lands, waters and facilities
18 that are under the jurisdiction and control of the secretary through
19 ownership, lease, license, cooperative agreement, memorandum of
20 understanding or other arrangement, including, but not limited to, state
21 parks, state lakes, recreational grounds, wildlife areas, sanctuaries, fish
22 hatcheries, natural areas and historic sites;
23 (2) "migratory waterfowl" means any wild goose, duck or merganser;
24 and
25 (3) "nonresident" means any person who has not been a bona fide
26 resident of this state for the immediately preceding 60 days. "Nonresident"
27 does not include:
28 (A) A nonresident lifetime license holder pursuant to K.S.A. 32-930,
29 and amendments thereto; or
30 (B) a nonresident entitled to resident licenses, permits, stamps and
31 other issues as authorized by K.S.A. 32-980, and amendments thereto.
32 (b) (1) Except as otherwise provided by law or rules and regulations
33 of the secretary and in addition to any other license, permit or stamp
34 required by law or rules and regulations of the secretary, a valid state
35 migratory waterfowl habitat stamp is required to hunt any migratory
36 waterfowl in this state.
SB 213 2
1 (c)(2) The provisions of subsection (b) paragraph (1) do not apply to
2 hunting, by legal means, by a person not required by K.S.A. 32-919, and
3 amendments thereto, to hold a hunting license.
4 (d)(c) The stamp required by this section is valid throughout the state.
5 (e)(d) The stamp required by this section is valid from the date of
6 issuance and expires at 12 midnight on June 30 following its the issuance
7 of such stamp.
8 (f)(e) Each migratory waterfowl habitat stamp shall be validated by
9 the signature of the stamp holder written across the face of such stamp.
10 (f) The fee for a waterfowl stamp shall be the amount prescribed
11 pursuant to K.S.A. 32-988, and amendments thereto.
12 (g) Nonresidents shall not hunt or take migratory waterfowl except
13 on Sundays, Mondays and Tuesdays during any established hunting
14 season for migratory waterfowl.
15 (h) The restriction set forth by subsection (g) shall apply to:
16 (1) Department lands and waters;
17 (2) federal lands and waters owned by the United States and
18 administered by:
19 (A) The secretary of the army, United States army corps of engineers
20 at:
21 (i) The Big Hill, Clinton, Council Grove, El Dorado, Elk City, Fall
22 River, Hillsdale, John Redmond, Kanopolis, Marion, Melvern, Milford,
23 Perry, Pomona, Toronto, Tuttle Creek and Wilson reservoirs; and
24 (ii) Missouri river fish and wildlife mitigation lands, including, but
25 not limited to, Benedictine Bottoms, Burr Oak, Dalbey, Elwood and Oak
26 Mills;
27 (B) the United States department of the interior, bureau of
28 reclamation at Cedar Bluff, Cheney, Glen Elder, Lovewell, Norton and
29 Webster reservoirs; and
30 (C) the United States department of the interior, United States fish
31 and wildlife service at Flint Hills national wildlife refuge, Quivira
32 national refuge, Marais Des Cygnes national wildlife refuge and Kirwin
33 national wildlife refuges.
34 (i) The restriction set forth by subsection (g) shall not apply to:
35 (1) Property enrolled and designated as either a walk-in hunting
36 access area or an interactive walk-in hunting access area;
37 (2) navigable rivers in Kansas; or
38 (3) the conservation order for light geese season as provided in 50
39 C.F.R. ยง 21.180.
