Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Welfare Reform
Requested by Representative Awerkamp
2-4
1 AN ACT concerning public assistance; relating to medical assistance;
2 requiring approval by an act of the legislature prior to any state agency
3 seeking or implementing a medicaid state plan, state plan amendment,
4 state demonstration or waiver under section 1115 or 1915 of the federal
5 social security act that expands coverage or increases cost to the state.
6
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
8 Section 1. (a) On and after July 1, 2025, no state agency shall seek or
9 implement a medicaid state plan, state plan amendment, state
10 demonstration or waiver pursuant to section 1115 or 1915 of the federal
11 social security act that would expand coverage to any additional
12 individuals or class of individuals or would increase any cost to the state
13 unless the legislature expressly consents to, and approves of, such
14 medicaid state plan, state plan amendment, state demonstration or waiver
15 by an act of the legislature. The provisions of this paragraph shall not
16 affect any state plan amendment or waiver program already being
17 implemented prior to July 1, 2025.
18 (b) For purposes of this section, "state agency" means the same as
19 defined in K.S.A. 75-3701, and amendments thereto.
20 Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
21 publication in the statute book.
