Section 1:
As Amended by Senate Committee
Session of 2025
By Committee on Government Efficiency
1-29
1 AN ACT concerning the state budget; relating to full-time positions;
2 requiring the head of each state agency to certify the number of full-
3 time positions paid from the state general fund that have been vacant
4 for more than 180 calendar days and abolishing such positions; lapsing
5 each account of the state general fund that is appropriated for the fiscal
6 year ending June 30, 2026, by the amount of the salaries and wages for
7 such abolished vacant positions.
8
9 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
10 Section 1. (a) On or before June 30, 2025, the head of each state
11 agency and the director of the budget shall consult and jointly certify to the
12 secretary of administration director of accounts and reports the number of
13 vacant positions in each such state agency paid from appropriations from
14 the state general fund that have been vacant for more than 180 calendar
15 days as of June 30, 2025. Upon receipt of each such certification, the
16 secretary of administration shall abolish the certified vacant positions on
17 July 1, 2025. Such abolished positions shall be deleted from the budget
18 documents and reports prepared by the director of the budget, the governor
19 or the legislative research department, including all related legislative
20 reports and measures submitted to the legislature.
21 (b) On July 1, 2025, the amount in each account of the state general
22 fund of each state agency that is appropriated for the fiscal year ending
23 June 30, 2026, by any appropriation act of the 2025 regular session of the
24 legislature, and that is budgeted for payment of salaries and wages,
25 including associated fringe benefits, of such abolished vacant positions, as
26 certified by the director of the budget to the director of accounts and
27 reports for fiscal year 2026, is hereby lapsed from each such account.
28 When the director of the budget transmits each such certification to the
29 director of accounts and reports pursuant to this section, the director of the
30 budget shall transmit a copy of such certification to the director of
31 legislative research.
32 (c) As used in this section, "state agency" means any state agency
33 named in an appropriation act of the 2025 regular session of the
34 legislature.
35 Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
36 publication in the Kansas register.
