Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Public Health and Welfare
2-17
1 AN ACT concerning health professions and practice; relating to the
2 regulation of nursing; authorizing registered nurse anesthetists to
3 prescribe, procure and administer drugs consistent with the nurse's
4 education and qualifications; amending K.S.A. 65-1158 and repealing
5 the existing section.
6
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
8 Section 1. K.S.A. 65-1158 is hereby amended to read as follows: 65-
9 1158. (a) Upon the order of a physician or dentist requesting anesthesia or
10 analgesia care, each registered nurse anesthetist shall be authorized to:
11 (1) Conduct a pre- and post-anesthesia and pre- and post-analgesia
12 visit and assessment with appropriate documentation;
13 (2) develop a general plan of anesthesia care with the physician or
14 dentist;
15 (3) select the method for administration of anesthesia or analgesia;
16 (4) select or administer appropriate medications and anesthetic agents
17 during the peri-anesthetic or peri-analgesic period;
18 (5) order necessary medications and tests in the peri-anesthetic or
19 peri-analgesic period;
20 (6) induce and maintain anesthesia or analgesia at the required levels;
21 (7) support life functions during the peri-anesthetic or peri-analgesic
22 period;
23 (8) recognize and take appropriate action with respect to patient
24 responses during the peri-anesthetic or peri-analgesic period;
25 (9) manage the patient's emergence from anesthesia or analgesia; and
26 (10) participate in the life support of the patient.
27 (b) (1) A registered nurse anesthetist may prescribe durable medical
28 equipment and prescribe, procure and administer any drug consistent with
29 the registered nurse anesthetist's education and qualifications. Except as
30 permitted by subsection (a), a registered nurse anesthetist shall not
31 prescribe, procure or administer any anesthetic agent. Any drug that is a
32 controlled substance shall be prescribed, procured or administered in
33 accordance with the uniform controlled substance act.
34 (2) A registered nurse anesthetist shall not perform or induce an
35 abortion or prescribe, procure or administer drugs for an abortion.
36 (3) A prescription order shall include the name, address and
Sub SB 67 2
1 telephone number of the registered nurse anesthetist. A registered nurse
2 anesthetist shall not dispense drugs but may request, receive and sign for
3 professional samples and may distribute professional samples to patients.
4 (4) In order to prescribe controlled substances, a registered nurse
5 anesthetist shall:
6 (A) Register with the federal drug enforcement administration; and
7 (B) comply with federal drug enforcement administration
8 requirements related to controlled substances.
9 (c) Each registered nurse anesthetist may participate in periodic and
10 joint evaluation of services rendered, including, but not limited to, chart
11 reviews, case reviews, patient evaluation and outcome of case statistics.
12 (c)(d) A registered nurse anesthetist shall perform duties and
13 functions in an interdependent role as a member of a physician or dentist
14 directed health care healthcare team.
15 Sec. 2. K.S.A. 65-1158 is hereby repealed.
16 Sec. 3. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
17 publication in the statute book.
