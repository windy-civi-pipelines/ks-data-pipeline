Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Committee on Local Government
Requested by Representative Featherston
1-28
1 AN ACT concerning firearms; relating to municipal regulation thereof;
2 authorizing cities and counties to regulate the sale and purchase of
3 firearms and ammunition within a building owned by such city or
4 county; amending K.S.A. 12-16,124 and repealing the existing section.
5
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
7 Section 1. K.S.A. 12-16,124 is hereby amended to read as follows:
8 12-16,124. (a) No city or county shall adopt or enforce any ordinance,
9 resolution or regulation, and no agent of any city or county shall take any
10 administrative action, governing the requirement of fees, licenses or
11 permits for, the commerce in or the sale, purchase, transfer, ownership,
12 storage, carrying, transporting or taxation of firearms or ammunition, or
13 any component or combination thereof.
14 (b) Any ordinance, resolution or regulation prohibited by subsection
15 (a) that was adopted prior to July 1, 2015, shall be null and void.
16 (c) Nothing in this section shall:
17 (1) Prohibit a city or county from adopting and enforcing any
18 ordinance, resolution or regulation relating to the personnel policies of
19 such city or county and the carrying of firearms by employees of such city
20 or county, except that any such ordinance, resolution or regulation shall
21 comply with the provisions of K.S.A. 75-7c01 et seq., and amendments
22 thereto;
23 (2) prohibit a city or county from adopting any ordinance, resolution
24 or regulation pursuant to K.S.A. 75-7c20, and amendments thereto;
25 (3) prohibit a law enforcement officer, as defined in K.S.A. 22-2202,
26 and amendments thereto, from acting within the scope of such officer's
27 duties; or
28 (4) prohibit a city or county from levying and collecting any retailers'
29 sales tax on the sale of firearms, ammunition or any component or
30 combination thereof as authorized by K.S.A. 12-189, and amendments
31 thereto; or
32 (5) prohibit a city or county from adopting and enforcing any
33 ordinance, resolution or regulation relating to the sale or purchase of
34 firearms or ammunition, or any component or combination thereof, within
35 any building owned by such city or county.
HB 2127 2
1 Sec. 2. K.S.A. 12-16,124 is hereby repealed.
2 Sec. 3. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
3 publication in the statute book.
