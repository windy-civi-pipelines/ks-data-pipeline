As Amended by House Committee
Session of 2025
By Committee on Commerce, Labor and Economic Development

1 AN ACT concerning economic development; relating to government
2 transparency; requiring local governments to report certain local
3 economic development incentive program information to the secretary
4 of commerce; defining such programs; requiring the secretary of
5 commerce to post such information on the economic development
6 incentive program database maintained by the secretary; requiring
7 certain search result presentation formats, a comprehensive report and a
8 summary report; amending K.S.A. 2024 Supp. 74-50,226 and 74-
9 50,227 and repealing the existing sections.
10
11 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:

13 follows: 74-50,226. As used in K.S.A. 2024 Supp. 74-50,226 and 74-
14 50,227, and amendments thereto:

16 charged with administering a particular economic development incentive

21 wholly or in part by the secretary of commerce;
22 (2) any tax credit program, except for social and domestic tax credits,
23 regardless of the administering agency;

32 Second, and amendments thereto; and
33 (5) any economic development fund, including, but not limited to, the

7 thereto;

9 amendments thereto;
10 (D) self-supported municipal improvement districts, K.S.A. 12-1794

35 or unified government, that is funded in whole or in part by such local
36 government.

22 imposed pursuant to K.S.A. 40-252, and amendments thereto, or the
23 privilege tax as measured by net income of financial institutions imposed

27 follows: 74-50,227. (a) The department of commerce shall collect
28 incentive data from economic development incentive programs that
29 provide more than $50,000 of annual incentives from administering
30 agencies or local governments as required by this section. Such data shall
31 be collected from administering agencies or local governments and be
32 stored in a database that is available to the public in a digital format. The
33 database shall contain information from multiple years and must be
34 searchable, printable and available to access over the internet on the
35 department of commerce's website on a permanently accessible web page
36 that may be accessed via a conspicuous link to that web page placed on the
37 front page of the department's website. Information included in the
38 database shall be updated by the department of commerce at least on an
39 annual basis and such update shall be completed prior to the end of the
40 following fiscal year in which such incentive was earned or distributed.
41 The database shall be a searchable website or web page that is
42 comprehensive of all information required by this section for all years as
43 required by this section and shall permit searches by a user of such

1 information by economic development incentive program, county and
2 recipient. The database shall permit the user, on one web page and by
3 means of an easily accessible drop-down menu or other similar prompt, to
4 select to search at least by keyword or phrase within separately identified
5 categories of economic development incentive program, county and
6 recipient name. The database shall be capable of calculating total
7 incentives for each category claimed by year and be searchable by
8 year. A search result shall include all information required by this section,
9 and such information applicable to the search result shall be in one
10 printable or downloadable report. In addition to such a comprehensive
11 report, the database shall be configured to provide a summary report in
12 response to a search when requested. The summary report shall provide
13 the total incentives awarded to the recipient, the number of years that
14 the incentive may be claimed, the total unencumbered incentive award

16 claimed by the recipient per year. Such information shall be produced by
17 economic development incentive program, county and recipient name.

19 commerce, labor and economic development and the senate committee
20 on commerce on or before January 31 of each year and shall disclose
21 the most recent three years of economic incentives claimed and the
22 total amount of funds committed by the state or the local government
23 that are required to be paid as an incentive over the entire period of
24 the incentive.

26 with all available and reasonably obtainable information required by this
27 section for all active economic development incentive programs of such
28 local government commenced prior to July 1, 2025, that provide more

40 shall provide updates of all applicable information required by this section
41 to the secretary of commerce, in the manner and form as required by the
42 secretary, at least annually and at such additional time or times as may be
43 required by the secretary.
