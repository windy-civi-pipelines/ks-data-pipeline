Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Senator Titus
1-3
1 AN ACT exempting the state of Kansas from daylight saving time;
2 providing for moving to permanent daylight saving time upon an act of
3 Congress requiring such move.
4
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
6 Section 1. (a) Commencing after 2 o'clock antemeridian on the first
7 Sunday of November of 2025, the state of Kansas shall not observe
8 daylight saving time and shall be exempt from the daylight saving time
9 provisions of 15 U.S.C. § 260a(a).
10 (b) This section shall not affect the standard time established by
11 United States law governing the movements of common carriers engaged
12 in interstate commerce.
13 (c) This section shall not affect the following times as may be
14 established by a statute, order, rule or regulation of the United States or an
15 agency thereof:
16 (1) The time for performance of an act by an officer or department of
17 the United States;
18 (2) the time within which any rights shall accrue or determine; or
19 (3) the time within which any act shall or shall not be performed by
20 any person subject to the jurisdiction of the United States.
21 (d) This section shall have no force and effect from and after the date
22 of publication in the Kansas register of the notice prescribed in section 2,
23 and amendments thereto.
24 Sec. 2. (a) The secretary of state shall monitor the enactment of any
25 federal law that requires the year-round observation of central daylight
26 saving time. If such law is enacted, the secretary shall cause a notice of
27 such enactment to be published in the Kansas register at the time of the
28 next regular publication of the Kansas register following the date such law
29 becomes effective.
30 (b) As used in this section, "central daylight saving time" means the
31 standard time of the third zone, as established in 15 U.S.C. § 261,
32 advanced one hour.
33 Sec. 3. (a) Commencing after 2 o'clock antemeridian on the first
34 Sunday of November after the effective date of this section, all areas of the
35 state of Kansas shall observe central daylight saving time.
36 (b) This section shall not affect the standard time established by
SB 1 2
1 United States law governing the movements of common carriers engaged
2 in interstate commerce.
3 (c) This section shall not affect the following times as may be
4 established by a statute, order, rule or regulation of the United States or an
5 agency thereof:
6 (1) The time for performance of an act by an officer or department of
7 the United States;
8 (2) the time within which any rights shall accrue or determine; or
9 (3) the time within which any act shall or shall not be performed by
10 any person subject to the jurisdiction of the United States.
11 (d) As used in this section, "central daylight saving time" means the
12 standard time of the third zone, as established in 15 U.S.C. § 261,
13 advanced one hour.
14 Sec. 4. Section 3 shall take effect and be in force from and after its
15 publication in the statute book and the date of publication in the Kansas
16 register of the notice prescribed in section 2.
17 Sec. 5. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
18 publication in the statute book.
