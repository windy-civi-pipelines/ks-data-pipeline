Section 1:
Session of 2025
By Senator Holscher
1-30
1 AN ACT concerning education; relating to the state board of education;
2 providing for reimbursements to local school boards for reduced price
3 school meals; prohibiting local school boards from collecting money
4 for such meals; amending K.S.A. 72-17,137 and repealing the existing
5 section.
6
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
8 Section 1. K.S.A. 72-17,137 is hereby amended to read as follows:
9 72-17,137. (a) Each board shall be entitled to receive, from appropriations
10 from the state general fund, six cents (6¢) $.06 for each type-A meal
11 served under an approved school lunch program during each school year
12 commencing with the 1978-79 school year.
13 (b) Commencing in the 2025-2026 school year:
14 (1) Each board shall be entitled to receive, from appropriations from
15 the state general fund, $.40 for each meal served under an approved
16 reduced price school meals program being provided in accordance with
17 42 U.S.C. 1759(a); and
18 (2) no board shall collect $.40 for such reduced price school meals
19 from students participating in the reduced price school meals program.
20 (c) For the purpose of this section every type-A lunch served shall be
21 counted equally. If the total amount of entitlements of all boards in any
22 school year is more than the amount appropriated for payment thereof,
23 then the entire amount appropriated therefor shall be prorated among all
24 boards in proportion to the amount each board is entitled to receive under
25 this act.
26 (b)(d) Moneys received by a board from appropriations from the state
27 general fund under the provisions of this section shall not be used for the
28 purchase of identifiable imported meats. As used in this subsection,
29 "identifiable imported meats" shall mean means meats which are labeled
30 as being imported.
31 Sec. 2. K.S.A. 72-17,137 is hereby repealed.
32 Sec. 3. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
33 publication in the statute book.
